## Tools I Use

### Job Request Platforms

To land freelance jobs, I use platforms like **Upwork** and **Fiverr**. These sites make it easy to browse job listings, send proposals, and connect with potential clients.

### Communication Tools

For staying in touch with clients, I use a mix of tools depending on what the client prefers—**WhatsApp**, **Facebook**, **Twitter**, or **email** are my go-to options.

### Requirement Gathering & Clarification

I often use **Google Forms** to collect system requirements from clients. It's a simple way to ensure I have all the necessary information in one place before starting a project.

### AI Writing Tools

AI tools have become part of my daily workflow. My favorites are **ChatGPT** and **Claude**—they help me brainstorm, write content, and improve clarity in documents.

### Payment Tools

When it comes to payments, the tools I use depend on the client. If they’re international, I go with platforms that support cross-border transactions. Otherwise, I opt for local solutions.
